About me

My research interests are about machine learning and natural language generation. Recently, I'm working on:

  • Trustful and creative text generation [2,3,5,6,7,9,12,13,16]
  • Viewing text/vision in a Physics atomic world [18]
  • Reinforcement learning utilities [2,3,4,17]

I'm currently a computer science PhD candidate at University of California, Santa Barbara (UCSB), working with professor William Yang Wang. Before joining UCSB, I received BS degree in Electrical Engineering from National Taiwan University, where I worked with professor Lin-shan Lee on speech processing, professor Hung-yi Lee on generative adversarial nets and reinforcement learning on sequence generation, and professor Yun-Nung (Vivian) Chen on conversational AI.
